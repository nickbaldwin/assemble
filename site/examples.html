Panels

Every "module" on this page is constructed from the same "partial", the only difference is the data supplied to each one from module.json.

The craziest thing about this is that I didn't (really) have to massacre the partial to make it work. I took liberties for the progress bars mostly, but considering how sparse the template is I don't feel too bad about it.
